"Purity is not a feature — it is our founding promise."

In Ambalavayal, nestled in the Western Ghats of Wayanad, coconut groves grow at altitudes that gift the fruit extraordinary richness. We source only from these select farms, where traditional cultivation meets clean, pesticide-free earth.

Every batch is cold-pressed within hours of harvest, preserving the lauric acid content, antioxidants, and the subtle creamy fragrance that only truly fresh coconut oil carries. No heat. No chemicals. No compromise.

How It's Made

The ancient art of cold pressing

Our process honours a centuries-old Kerala tradition, with care and patience at every step. No shortcuts — only craftsmanship.

01
Hand Harvested

Mature coconuts hand-selected at peak ripeness from highland groves at 900m altitude in Wayanad.

02
Fresh Extraction

Coconut meat grated and milk extracted within hours of opening — before any oxidation can occur.

03
Cold Pressing

Pressed at below 40°C — the threshold that preserves enzymes, nutrients, and natural fragrance.

04
Bottled & Sealed

Gravity-settled and hand-poured into glass — sealed to lock in freshness from light and air.

My Story · Founder

A Note From Shahil


Khaair began with a simple belief — everyone deserves access to truly pure coconut oil without paying an unreasonable price.

Growing up in Kerala, where coconut oil has been a part of daily life for generations, I learned to appreciate the difference that genuine quality makes. It is more than a product; it is a tradition, a source of nourishment, and a part of our heritage.

Purity should not be a luxury. It should be the standard.

As I explored the market, I noticed that many products were either heavily processed, compromised in quality, or sold at prices that did not reflect their true value. I knew there had to be a better way.

That vision became Khaair — a brand built on honesty, transparency, and uncompromising quality. Every bottle is carefully crafted to preserve the natural goodness of coconut oil while delivering exceptional value to families.

Every bottle carries a promise — authentic purity, premium quality, and trust you can feel in every drop.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Your trust inspires us to continue delivering coconut oil the way nature intended.

Founder, Khaair

Why It Matters

A tradition of nourishment & care

Deep Nourishment

Rich in lauric acid and medium-chain fatty acids, penetrating deeply to nourish skin, hair, and body from within — a daily ritual of care.

Culinary Excellence

With a naturally high smoke point and unmistakably fresh coconut aroma, it elevates Kerala's traditional dishes to their most authentic expression.

Antioxidant Rich

Cold pressing preserves natural polyphenols and vitamin E — giving our oil its subtle golden hue and powerful protective properties.

Immune Support

The lauric acid in coconut oil converts to monolaurin in the body — a powerful compound that strengthens natural defences.

Ayurvedic Heritage

Used in Ayurveda for millennia — for oil pulling, abhyanga massage, and balancing the body's natural constitution across all seasons.
